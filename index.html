About Me:

I was born in Somalia in the fall of 1988. I don't remember much of Somalia. My parents fled Somalia after the start of Somalia's devestating civil war in 1991. My parents had seven children. I was the third to last. In the midst of the civil war, my mother lost the two youngest children of the family. I became the youngest of the remaining five children. Little did I knew the magnitude of the loss my parents were suffering. As a child myself, all I cared about was playing with other children. After several more years of living in Ethiopia, a neighboring country, my family were among the lucky few that were given refugee status by the United States government. We were flown out of Ethiopia and to San Diego, California in 1996. I grew up in San Diego and have not left ever since.

My Hobbies:

Despite the many hours it takes coding and the amount of frustration one experiences, in the end of the day, it is one of the most fun things to do. For some, it is just a job, for other, it becomes a hobby.
